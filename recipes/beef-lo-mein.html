Beef Lo mein

Description

Just like you order out, this tasty lo mein will be the perfect meal prep and quick, easy dinner to whip together on a busy night. Use your leftover veggies and meat, and make it a house special!

Ingredients

  • Lo Mein Noodles
  • Beef
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Honey
  • Siracha
  • Veggies of your choosing

Steps

  1. Mix the Oyster Sauce, Sesame Oil, Soy Sauce, Honey, and Siracha together for the Lo Mein Sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Cook the Steak
  4. Cook the veggies
  5. Combine everything in a big pot and serve hot
