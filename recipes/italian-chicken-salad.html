Italian Chicken Salad

Description

Counting your calories? This meal is the perfect way to fill up and save room for another meal. Low calores, high flavor!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Filet
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber
  • Carrots
  • Balsamic Vinagrette

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken
  2. Chop the carrots, tomatoes, lettuce, and cucumbers."
  3. Mix all of the veggies on a plate
  4. Add Balsamic Vinagrette to the salad
  5. Put the chicken on the plate
