Creamy Chicken Gnocchi

Description

This delicious dish is the perfct cold-weather meal. It's warm, it's hearty, it's filling, and it's so tasty. Read below how to make it.

Ingredients

  • Dried Gnocchi
  • Chicken Breast
  • Spinach
  • Carrots, Onions, and Celery
  • Chicken Broth
  • Heavy Cream

Steps

  1. Dice the chicken, celery, carrots, and onion. Chop the Spinach.
  2. Cook the chicken so that it's slightly browned, then remove from heat.
  3. Put carrots, celery, and onion into large pot and cook with butter until the onions are transluscent and very aromatic.
  4. Add chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  5. Add gnocchi and let cook until nearly fully cooked (about 13 minutes)
  6. Add chicken and spinach, and let cook until the spinach is wilted and the chicken is fully cooked.
  7. Add heavy cream and stir.
  8. Serve hot!
